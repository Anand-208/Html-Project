Parts of Speech

In the english language, words can be considered as the smallest element that have distinctive meanings. based on their use and function, words can be categorize into several type of parts of speech.

Names of Parts of speech

There are eight parts of speech.

  1. Verb
  2. Noun
  3. Adjective
  4. Determiner
  5. Adverb
  6. Pronoun
  7. Preposition
  8. Conjunction
  9. Interjection
  1. Noun:-Noun is the name of person, place or things.
        Types of Noun
    1. proper Noun
    2. Common Noun
    3. Collective Noun
    4. Material Noun
    5. Abstract Noun
  2. Pronoun:-A pronoun is a part of speech which function as a noun.
      3. Ex:-it, he, she, his, hers, we, they, theirs, and ours.
  3. Adjective:-This parts os speech is used to describe a noun or a pronoun. Adjective can specify the quality the size, and the number of Noun or Pronoun.
  4. Verb:-This is the most important part of speech, for without a verb, a sentence would no exist. This is a word shows an action (physical or mental) or state of being of the subject in a sentence.
      Ex:-am, is, was, are, and were.
  5. Adverb:-Just like Adjective, adverbsare also used to describe word, but the difference is that adverbs describe adjective, verbs, or another adverb.
  6. Preposition:-This parts of speech basically refer to words that specify location or a location in time.
      7. Ex:-above, below, throughout, outside, before, near and since.
  7. Conjunction:-The conjunction is part of speech which joins words, pharase, clauses together.
      8. Ex:-
    1. and
    2. yet
    3. but
    4. for
    5. nor
    6. so
  8. Interjunction:-This part of speech refer to words which express emotion. since interjunction are commony used to convey strong emotions.
      9. Ex:-
    1. Ouch:That must have heart.
    2. Hurray!We won!
    3. Hey!I sais enough.
